I am an artist, designer, and keen observer with a passion for detail. My journey began in architecture, where I developed a deep understanding of user experience in the physical realm. Now, as a User Interface Designer, I channel that knowledge into the digital landscape, creating innovative designs that relates with the users. My unique blend of creativity and technology allows me to design, code, illustrate, and paint. I also explore my artistic expression through tattooing, using human skin as a canvas in my spare time. This diverse skill set exemplifies my belief that "design skills are transferable." I strive to empathize with users on a deeper level, ensuring my designs not only look good but also enhance the overall experience. With each project, I push the boundaries of design, aiming to create meaningful and impactful experiences to be able to transform lives through design!
